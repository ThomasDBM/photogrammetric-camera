Image Based Rendering of Micmac datasets

You can open Micmac files here using drag and drop :

  • Images: {name}.jpg files
  • Orientations: Orientation-{name}.{ext}.xml (orientations and images are matched by name)
  • Intrinsics: AutoCal*.xml (must be drag-and-dropped jointly with orientations that share its intrinsics)
  • PointClouds: *.ply (eg the AperiCloud)

The scene is composed of a large view-centered sphere (500m) and the loaded point clouds if any.

These are colored by the intrinsic colors of the pointcloud, replaced by the unprojection of the image from the current camera.

During camera animation, the image of the target camera is used.

Orientations with no images show a blue/red calibration texture.

Keyboard Actions :

  • b/n: move to previous/next image
  • -/+: decrease/increase point size
  • c: print the current camera in the console
